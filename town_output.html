The image above shows those residents with special relationships. A black line indicates a very good relationship while a double black line shows love or a strong alliance. A red line shows animosity while a double red line signifies deep hate. Both a black and a red line indicates a complex relationship. The residents that are not shown in the image does not have special relationships. Residents with the same surname has a good family relationship unless any lines shows an even better relationsip with black line(s) or bad relationsip with red line(s). The number at the bottom of each person (like #14) is the house where they live.

Right-click on the image and choose to download to save your town relations. If you are not satisfied with the generated relations, simply download the town file, go to the front page and use the tool to upload the town file to generate a new relationship map.

For large settlements, the map becomes chaotic, and then it is more helpful to read the relations in a plain text file. To do so, simply click the biutton "Download town relations". In the file, "+>" indicates a positive relationship, whereas "->" is a negative relationship.
